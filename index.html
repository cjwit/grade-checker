Grade Checker

What is this thing?

It is a tool to help you work out your plan for the semester. The first set of projects are required, but the second set are the ones you can choose between. Each is weighted differently. To ease the math, you can select the ones that you would like to do and play with figuring out what your scores need to be to get the grade that you want for the semester.

You are welcome to do as many or as few assignments as you need to get the score for the semester that you want to end up with. Be aware, though, that many of these projects have multiple stages and deadlines that come before the end of the semester, so you cannot wait until the end to wrap up a bunch of work at once.

This is for your own use. I cannot see what you type or do here, but I hope that it can be helpful. As the semester moves forward, you can put your actual grades in these boxes to figure out what you need to do moving forward. Directions for the individual projects are in Canvas.
