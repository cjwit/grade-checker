Grade Checker (MUCT1250)

What is this thing?

It is a tool to help you work out your plan for the semester. The first set of assignments are required, but the second set are the ones you can choose between. To ease the math, you can enter your grades from Canvas (or what you hope to get) to keep an eye on where your grade stands.

You are welcome to do as many or as few assignments as you need to get the score for the semester that you want to end up with. Be aware, though, that many of these projects have multiple stages and deadlines that come before the end of the semester, so you cannot wait until the end to wrap up a bunch of work at once.

This is for your own use. I cannot see what you type or do here, but I hope that it can be helpful. Directions for the individual projects are in Canvas.
